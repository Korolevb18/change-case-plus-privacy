Change Case Plus – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data Collection

Change Case Plus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or text content. All text processing happens locally on your device.

2. Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions for the sole purpose of its functionality:

  • contextMenus – to show the "Change Case Plus" menu when you right-click selected text.
  • clipboardWrite – to copy converted text to your clipboard when you apply a case change to non-editable text.
  • activeTab – to access the current tab's text selection and input fields when you trigger a conversion.

3. Third-Party Services

Change Case Plus does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any external servers or services.

4. Uninstall Feedback

When you uninstall the extension, a feedback page may open in your browser. Submitting feedback is optional. The feedback form sends your responses and browser language to a private server for the sole purpose of improving the extension. No personal identifiers are collected.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the support link in the Chrome Web Store listing.
